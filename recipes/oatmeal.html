Strawberries and Cream Oatmeal

Description

The breakfast of champions. Oatmeal is a staple breakfast due to being high in fiber and being a quick and easy thing to make in the morning.

Ingredients

  • diced strawberries
  • milk
  • water
  • brown sugar
  • salt
  • oatmeal
  • vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Blend strawberries, milk, water, brown sugar, and salt in a blender and blend until smooth.
  2. Place oatmeal and strawberry mixture into a saucepan.
  3. Bring to a boil.
  4. Reduce the heat, add remaining strawberries, and simmer until thick, about 5 minutes.
  5. Remove from heat.
  6. Stir in vanilla.
