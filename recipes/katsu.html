Chicken Katsu

Description

Chicken katsu is a Japanese-style fried chicken. It is best served with white rice and tonkatsu sauce.

Ingredients

  • chicken breasts
  • salt
  • pepper
  • all-purpose flour
  • eggs
  • panko bread crumbs
  • canola or vegetable oil

Steps

  1. Season chicken breasts on both sides with salt and pepper.
  2. Place flour, beaten egg, and panko crumbs into seperate shallow dishes.
  3. Coat chicken breasts in flour, shaking off any excess.
  4. Dip chicken into egg, then press into panko crumbs until well coated on both sides.
  5. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
  6. Fry chicken in hot oil until golden brown, 3 to 4 minutes per side.
  7. Transfer to a paper towel-lined plate to drain.
